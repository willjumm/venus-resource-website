[02] DEVELOPMENT THESIS // KORELLA

Highlights

The Korella Phosphate Project is a near-term, low-complexity DSO development opportunity located in Northwest Queensland, with a clear pathway to production and initial shipment within a short timeframe.

[01]

Mining Concept

Simple open-cut, free-dig operation requiring no drill and blast. Significantly reduces technical complexity, development risk, and capital intensity.

[02]

Production Strategy

Prioritises early extraction of high-grade DSO phosphate, enabling rapid monetisation and early cash flow generation through staged production.

[03]

Execution Readiness

Progressed beyond conceptual stage with detailed mine scheduling completed and contractor engagement underway, including integrated mining services tender preparation.

[04]

Timeline & Deliverability

Initial production expected within a ~6-month execution window, aligned with seasonal operating constraints, demonstrating a clear and achievable path to first shipment.

[05]

Low-Risk Development Profile

Fully permitted with Environmental Authority in place. Proven resource grades from trial mining (~22kt extracted at 28.5-35% P2O5). Minimal upfront capex through contractor-led DSO model with clear line of sight to revenue.
