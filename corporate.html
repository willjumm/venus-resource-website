[01] MISSION STATEMENT

About Australia Venus Resource

Founded in 2014, Australia Venus Resource (AVR) has emerged as a cornerstone of strategic resource management in the Australian mining sector. We are committed to the disciplined exploration and development of high-value mineral assets that power both current industrial needs and the global energy transition.

Our flagship operation resides in the 100% ownership of the Corella Bore project. This premium asset represents our commitment to geological excellence and operational integrity, serving as the foundational pillar upon which our multi-commodity strategy is built. Through architectural precision in our mining practices, we ensure long-term value for stakeholders and the landscape alike.
